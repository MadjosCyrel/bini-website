About Bini

Bini is a popular Filipino girl group formed in 2021. Known for their catchy songs and energetic performances, they inspire fans with messages of empowerment and positivity. The group connects closely with their audience, called "BLOOM," through social media and engaging content. With their unique style and talent, Bini is on the rise in the music industry!
